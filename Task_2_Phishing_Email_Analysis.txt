Dear User,

This is an automated message from the Microsoft Office 365 Security Team.

We have detected unusual sign-in activity associated with your Office 365 account.
For your protection, access to your account will be temporarily limited unless you
verify your identity immediately.

Failure to verify within 24 hours will result in permanent suspension of your email,
contacts, and stored files.

Verify My Account Now

Thank you,
Microsoft 365 Security Team






Phishing Indicators Identified
1.The sender domain support-offices365-update.com does not belong to any official Microsoft domain and indicates a domain impersonation attempt.
2.The email employs urgency and fear-inducing language, such as “URGENT” and “Final Notice”, to pressure the recipient into immediate action.
3.Email header analysis reveals that the message originated from an unknown IP address, which is atypical for legitimate Microsoft communications.
4.The absence or failure of standard email authentication mechanisms, including SPF, DKIM, and DMARC, suggests the email was not sent by an authorized source.
5.The email uses a generic salutation (“Dear User”) instead of addressing the recipient by name, a common characteristic of phishing emails.
